  • Benefits of Blood Donation


  • Helps to save lives:
    Blood donation is vital for patients who need blood
    transfusions due to accidents, surgeries,or medical
    conditions.

  • Reduces the risk of heart disease:
    Regular blood donation can help reduce the risk of heart
    disease by lowering the levels of iron in the blood.

  • Provides a free health check-up:
    Before donating blood, you will be screened for various
    health conditions such as blood pressure, hemoglobin levels,
    and infectious diseases.

  • Gives a sense of purpose:
    Donating blood can give you a sense of purpose and fulfillment
    knowing that you are helping someone in need.
